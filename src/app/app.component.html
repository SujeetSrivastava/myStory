Childhood & Schooling:

There is always something special about childhood, because it gives the shape of your character and lays the foundation of your inner strength.

My Childhood begin with the bitter memory of loosing my father. When I lost my father I was 4 year old so I missed being a little kid with no stress and worries in the world. I missed him throughout my childhood but then I choose to lookup my mother who at the very tender age 24 was left with four children to look after. My mother is a teacher and I am third in number out of four children. Early departure of my father from our life taught me the toughest lesson of my life from survival to achievement.

Amid all challenge I took up my primary and secondary education from small and humble school in Jaunpur...

Adolescence to Adulthood:

By my experience I would say that this is a stage of self discovery. Around this time I was trying to sought the answers to bundle of questions related to my career and my future.

My elder brother played a major role in giving me the direction regarding how I am going to pursue my career. He was more like a teacher than a brother. I decided to explore my fortune in IIT. I remember having worked really hard and burnt midnight oil to crack IIT. But I failed :( in my attempt.

I was good at studies but could not crack IIT, some people said your are destined to be somewhere else but I don't believe in destiny. For me destiny is the word for week and sort of cheap psychiatry to cover up one's failure, may be some lack of strategy that led me to failed outcomes.

I took the full responsibility, gathered strengths and moved on...

By chance I got my career in Computer Application. I completed my Bachelors from Lucknow & Masters from Apeejay College, greater Noida, India.

Very smoothly I cleared my Bachelors and Masters, but the battle within was on until I got my first break.


(Apeejay Institute of technology, Gr.Noida, India)
